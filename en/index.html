FAQ

Before you write in, this might already answer it

Why hire a freelancer instead of a big consultancy?

No sales layers or middle management: you talk to me from the first email through report delivery, which means a tighter price without losing technical rigor.

How is confidentiality of my data guaranteed?

We sign an NDA before any work begins. All findings and evidence are handled under GDPR and deleted at project close if you request it.

What if you don't find any vulnerabilities?

You still receive a report with the methodology applied and scope covered — useful as evidence of due diligence for clients, insurers or compliance audits.

When and how do I pay?

Typically 50% when confirming the engagement and 50% on report delivery. For smaller projects, a single payment on delivery. No surprises: the price is fixed before starting.

Do you only work in Cádiz, or across Spain?

100% remote across Spain. The initial consultation and results presentation are done by video call, no travel required.

Do you also fix the vulnerabilities you find?

The service covers detecting, documenting and prioritizing. Your technical team implements the fix, but I can support the process and validate that the fix closes the issue.
